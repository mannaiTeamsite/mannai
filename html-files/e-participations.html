About E-Participation

The Qatar Digital Government 2020 Strategy is about simple, fast and secure services, anywhere, anytime. The strategy focuses on people, using technology to deliver real benefits to the people of Qatar - to members of the public, whether they are citizens, residents or visitors; businesses and private organizations; and government entities themselves. Qatar Digital Government 2020 strategy aims to enhance the customer experience, provide greater access to government services, and improve government efficiency, leading to better outcomes for the nation. The strategy has one clear vision: “All individuals and businesses will benefit from connecting online with Qatar's more open and efficient government.” To deliver this vision, the strategy has three objectives:

1st Objective Better serve individuals and business 2nd Objective Create efficiency in government administration 3rd Objective Increase government openness

E-Participation is part of the third objective – “Increase Government Openness”. Public participation and citizen engagement are vital to shape public policies and determine public service delivery. The overall objective of the government is to encourage and provide channels for digital civic engagement that lead the country to make higher quality, more relevant decisions and policies that have a greater degree of public buy-in. It has been found that an increasing number of youth are online and have a desire to contribute to the policymaking process through digital (online) channels, and in the absence of formal channels are expressing their opinions through informal channels (e.g., Twitter). The overall objective of the government is to encourage and provide channels for digital civic engagement that lead the country to make higher quality, more relevant decisions and policies that have a greater degree of public buy-in. E-Participation will enable the public to participate more broadly and deeply in the policy making process across all levels of e-participation to create a more active public. Furthermore, Qatar’s National Vision 2030 aims to increase the limited engagement of citizens in Government related issues, and as such, e-participation will be one of the major initiatives to help establish Qatar’s reputation as an e-government leader and boost the country’s position in the global e-participation rankings. The e-Participation Policy can be checked through the website of the Ministry of Transport and Communications.
