Entity Services’ Statistics

Qatar e-Government portal - Hukoomi - is keen on promoting the transparency between the government and its citizens, as part of the fruitful efforts of the Ministry of Transport and Communications to apply the open data policy, which is an essential part of Qatar National Vision 2030. For this purpose, we have combined all the necessary statistics related to the services provided to us by various entities in Qatar and that are available on Hukoomi, as per the following table. Remark: When calculating the percentage of E-Services for each entity, the number of Information Services was deducted from the total number of services.

Qatar Museums 288 23 8 23 80%
