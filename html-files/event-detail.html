About

On behalf of the Organizing and Scientific Committee of Qatar Health 2020, it gives us great pleasure to welcome you to this international scientific gathering: Qatar Health 2020. Qatar Health 2020 is a collaborative effort between Hamad Medical Corporation and the Ministry of Public Health in preparation for the FIFA World Cup 2022. It will be hosted in Qatar from 16 to 18 January 2020. The conference will be a multidisciplinary academic meeting open to healthcare professionals and experts from different backgrounds and countries. Content will be structured around to global mass gatherings for health care, disaster management and a trauma system approach to mass casualty events.
